Harry Potter

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Harry Potter is a young wizard/student at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry's story consists of an enemy named Lord Voldemort who tries to kill Harry throughout multiple storybook sequences. Harry's friends, Ronald Weasley and Hermione Granger, help Harry battle injustices suffered in the wizard world. Throughout his life, Harry has had to battle and balance the best of both worlds: magical and non-magical.
